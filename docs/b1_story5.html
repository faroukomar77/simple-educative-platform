The Cooking Mistake

Nina wanted to surprise her family with a homemade dinner. She found a recipe for lasagna online and bought all the ingredients. She followed the steps carefully, but she accidentally set the oven to the wrong temperature.

fter 40 minutes, the lasagna was still cold in the middle and slightly burnt on the top. Feeling disappointed, she told her family what happened. They all laughed and helped her fix it by heating it properly. Despite the mistake, everyone enjoyed the meal.

Nina realized that cooking is about learning, and even small mistakes can bring people together with laughter.
