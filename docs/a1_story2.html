Tom’s First Day at School

Tom is six years old, and today is a big day—it’s his first day at school. He wakes up early, brushes his teeth, and puts on his favorite blue shirt. His mother helps him pack his bag and gives him a big hug. At school, he meets his new teacher, Mrs. Green, who smiles warmly.

Tom feels shy but curious. He sits next to a girl named Sara, and they start talking. They draw pictures and play with blocks together. At lunch, Tom eats a sandwich and an apple. He enjoys talking with his new classmates. After school, he tells his parents, “I like school!” That night, he puts his backpack next to his bed, ready for tomorrow.
