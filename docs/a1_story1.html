Anna’s New Cat

Anna is a young girl who lives in a small house with her mother and father. She is ten years old and loves animals. One morning, while she is playing in the garden, she hears a soft sound near the flowers. When she looks closer, she sees a small white and grey cat hiding behind a tree. The cat looks scared and very hungry.

Anna slowly walks to the cat and gives it some milk from the kitchen. The cat drinks the milk and begins to feel safe. It lies down on the grass and falls asleep. Anna smiles and watches the cat sleep. She feels happy. She decides to keep the cat and calls it “Snow” because of its soft white fur.

Now, every day, Anna takes care of Snow. She feeds him, plays with him, and talks to him. Snow follows Anna everywhere she goes. They play in the garden and rest under the sun. Anna feels that Snow is her best friend. She is never lonely now. Snow is happy, and Anna is happy too.
