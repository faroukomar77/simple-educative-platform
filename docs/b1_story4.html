The Language Exchange Partner

Carlos wanted to improve his English speaking skills, so he joined an online language exchange program. He was paired with Lily, a girl from Canada who wanted to learn Spanish. At first, their conversations were a bit awkward because they were both shy. But week by week, they became more comfortable.

They discussed music, movies, and their daily lives. Carlos noticed his confidence growing, and Lily said her Spanish was improving too.
