About the course

Learning a new language can be challenging, but it doesn't have to be boring! Our course is designed to make Learning English fun and engaging through the use of stories. By reading and Listening to stories, you'll not only improve your vocabulary and grammar, but also develop your listening and reading comprehension skills.

Our stories are carefully selected to be interesting and relevant to your Life, so you can Learn English in a way that feels natural and enjoyable. Plus, with our interactive exercises and quizzes, you'll be able to practice what you've learned in a fun and engaging way for levels(A1,A2,B1).


Here’s how it works:


  1. Read One Story a Day

    2. Start by choosing a story that matches your level. Each story is short, fun, and filled with useful everyday vocabulary and grammar in context

  2. Read the Same Story 3 Times a Day

    3. To strengthen your memory, read the same story 3 Times a Day. Repetition helps your brain absorb and remember the language.

  3. Listen While You Read (Coming Soon!)

    4. For each story, you will be able to listen to the audio version — with native pronunciation and natural rhythm. Listening while reading boosts pronunciation, accent awareness, and comprehension.

  4. Repeat Daily for One Week

    5. This daily cycle creates a strong habit and helps you retain vocabulary long-term. You won’t just learn new words — you will remember them for life.
