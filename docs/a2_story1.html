Anna’s New City

Anna recently moved to a new city for her job. At first, everything felt strange—the streets, the people, and even the food. She missed her old friends and favorite coffee shop. One day, she decided to take a walk around her neighborhood. She discovered a small bookstore with a cozy café inside.

The owner smiled and invited her in. She spent an hour reading and drinking tea. A woman next to her started a conversation, and they quickly became friends. Now, Anna visits the bookstore every weekend. The city is starting to feel like home.
