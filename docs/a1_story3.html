The Lost Dog

Emma loves her dog Max. One sunny morning, they go to the park together. Max runs happily and chases butterflies. Suddenly, Max sees a squirrel and runs far away. Emma calls out, “Max! Come back!” but Max does not stop. Emma starts to worry. She looks behind trees, near benches, and asks people for help.

After almost one hour, she hears a small bark. She finds Max sitting under a tree, tired but safe. Emma hugs him and gives him water. She learns that Max needs to stay close. From that day, she always keeps his leash on during walks.
