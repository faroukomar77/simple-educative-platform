The Forgotten Phone

Mike was in a hurry to catch the bus to work. He grabbed his bag, locked the door, and ran. When he got on the bus, he reached into his pocket—no phone! He felt nervous. What if his boss called? What if he missed an important message? At work, he asked to use a colleague’s phone to call home.

Luckily, his sister answered and said she found the phone on the kitchen table. After work, Mike rushed home and thanked her. From that day, he always double-checked his things before leaving the house.
