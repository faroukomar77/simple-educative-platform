Sarah’s Birthday Cake

Sarah wakes up excited—it’s her birthday! She is turning eight today. Her mother is baking a chocolate cake with her. Sarah adds the flour and sugar while her mom mixes the batter. They put strawberries on top and write “Happy Birthday” with pink icing. In the afternoon, Sarah’s friends arrive.

They play games, dance to music, and laugh a lot. When it’s time for cake, everyone sings “Happy Birthday.” Sarah makes a wish and blows out the candles. The cake tastes amazing! She opens her gifts and smiles all day. It’s a birthday she will never forget.
