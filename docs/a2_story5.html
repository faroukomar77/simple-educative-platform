The English Class Presentation

Maria’s English teacher told the class they had to give a short presentation next week. Maria felt nervous—she had never spoken in front of a group in English before. She chose to talk about her favorite movie. She wrote her script, practiced every day, and asked her brother to help her

On the day of the presentation, her hands were shaking. But once she started talking, she felt more confident. Her classmates clapped, and the teacher said she did a great job. Maria felt proud of herself and less afraid to speak in English.
